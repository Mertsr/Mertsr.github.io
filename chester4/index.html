Building Chester V1

The journey to create a truly helpful humanoid service robot wasn't easy.
Here is how Chester evolved from a concept to reality.

Concept & Design

2024-2025

The "friendly face" philosophy is born. Early sketches focused on a non-threatening, square-headed design that emphasizes digital eyes for clear communication.

The "Chester" Prototype

2025

Chester V1 Development: Successfully engineered the first functional prototype utilizing a custom Lidar-Vision stack. This milestone proved high-level autonomous mobility on affordable hardware, validating our core technology.

FIELD TESTING & PILOT PROGRAMS

2026

Real-world deployment and validation. Launch of pilot programs with industry partners to test Chester V1 in live warehouse and retail environments, refining the autonomous navigation stack for unpredictable scenarios.

SCALE & GLOBAL REACH

2027

Commercial launch and European market expansion. Scaling operations from initial sales to full deployment in major international distribution centers.
